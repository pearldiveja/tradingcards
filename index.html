TRADING CARD VALUE GUIDE

STEP 1: What type of trading card do you have?

Pokémon Card: Age Check

When was the card produced?

Vintage Pokémon Card (1990s)

These cards have high potential value. Check for these features:

  • First Edition Stamp (small "1" in a circle on the left side of the card)
  • Shadowless (no shadow around the image box)
  • Holographic (shiny/reflective image)
  • Condition (corners, edges, scratches)

High Value Pokémon Card!

This card has significant potential value! Consider:

  1. Getting it professionally graded by PSA, BGS, or SGC
  2. Researching recent sales of similar cards
  3. Listing it individually on eBay or TCGPlayer
  4. Protecting it in a sleeve and toploader

Medium Value Pokémon Card

This card has moderate value potential. Consider:

  1. Checking recent sales on eBay or TCGPlayer
  2. Listing individually if worth more than $10
  3. Protecting in a sleeve and toploader
